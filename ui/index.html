💡 Wireless Connection Quick Guide

1

Enable Wireless Debugging

Go to settings > Developer options > Toggle "Wireless Debugging" ON.

2

Scan Network (mDNS)

Click "Scan Network" below to auto-discover your phone IP and connection port.

3

First-Time Pairing

Tap "Pair device with pairing code". Keep it open, enter its Pairing Port & Code on the right, and click "Pair Wireless Device".

4

Connect

Once paired, enter the connection port from the phone's main Wireless Debugging screen, and click "Connect Device".
